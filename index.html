Services

As a video game developer, my goal is to transform your ideas into visually appealing and playable projects, providing a complete experience from design to implementation.

Game Asset Creation

I create 2D visual assets for your games, including characters, settings and objects, all aligned with the aesthetics of your project.

Game Programing

I am in charge of programming in Unity, implementing game mechanics, interaction and performance optimization.

Visual Effects and Particles (VFX)

I design visual effects and particle systems to enhance game immersion, such as spells, explosions, and more.

Animation for Games

I create fluid and natural animations for characters, improving their movement and behavior in the game for a more immersive experience.

Environment and Scene Design

design and construction of detailed and dynamic scenarios, creating environments that complement the gameplay and narrative of your project.

Level Design

Challenging and engaging level design, ensuring a good flow and progression to keep the player engaged.
